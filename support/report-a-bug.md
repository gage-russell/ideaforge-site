# Report a bug or request a feature

Spotted a problem or have an idea that would make Ideaforge better? Send us a quick note and we’ll take a look.

## What to include

- What you were trying to do
- The exact steps you took
- What you expected vs. what happened
- Any error messages
- Screenshots or screen recordings (if possible)

## How to reach us

- Email: <a href="mailto:{{ site.email }}">{{ site.email }}</a>

We appreciate your feedback — it helps us improve Ideaforge.
