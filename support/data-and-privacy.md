# Data & privacy

Your privacy matters. Here’s where to find details and how to contact us with questions or requests.

## Privacy Policy

- Read our full Privacy Policy: <a href="{{ site.baseurl }}/privacy-policy.html">Privacy Policy</a>

## Account deletion

- You can delete your account from within the app at any time. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
- Step-by-step instructions: <a href="{{ site.baseurl }}/support/account/#delete-your-account">Delete your account</a>

## Data requests and questions

- For data access, correction, or deletion requests (beyond in‑app deletion), email us at <a href="mailto:{{ site.email }}">{{ site.email }}</a>.
- Include the email address associated with your account and a brief description of your request.
