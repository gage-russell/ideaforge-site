# User Profile

Find your profile from the bottom navigation, update your details, and log out when you're done.

## Open your profile

Use the bottom navigation to open the User tab. This shows your profile and account‑related actions.

<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/support/user_profile/user-profile.png" alt="User profile screen" width="360" />

## Edit your profile

Update your name or other profile details. Changes are saved to your account.

<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/support/user_profile/edit-profile.png" alt="Edit profile" width="360" />

## Log out

From the profile screen, choose Log out to securely sign out of the app.

<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/support/user_profile/logout.png" alt="Log out from profile" width="360" />
