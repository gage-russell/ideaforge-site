## Quick help

### Create an account and sign in
1. Open the app.
2. Tap “Register” on the login screen and complete the form.
3. Check your email for a verification link and tap it.
4. Return to the app and sign in.

<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/support/login.png" alt="Login screen" width="360" />

<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/support/register.png" alt="Register screen" width="360" />

Tip: If you can access the app after registering but features seem limited, tap the “User” tab at the bottom and confirm your email is verified. Some areas remain restricted until verification completes.

<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/support/email-not-verified.png" alt="User tab showing missing email verification state" width="360" />

### Reset your password
1. On the login screen, tap “Forgot Password?”.
2. Enter your email and submit.
3. Tap the password reset link in the email, then set a new password.

<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/support/reset-password.png" alt="Reset password form" width="360" />

### Delete your account
1. Open Settings → Account.
2. Scroll to “Danger Zone”.
3. Tap “Delete Account” and confirm.
4. You’ll be signed out and your account will be permanently removed.

## Troubleshooting

### Didn’t receive the verification/reset email?
- Check spam/junk folders and promotions tabs.
- Add our address to your contacts and request the email again.
- If using a corporate or school email, ensure external messages aren’t blocked.

### Can’t sign in after verifying?
- Close and reopen the app, then try again.
- Ensure you’re on the latest app version.
- Reset your password and attempt sign-in again.

### Network issues
- Use a reliable internet connection.
- If you’re on a restrictive network, try mobile data or another Wi‑Fi.
- The app requires secure HTTPS endpoints for production.

Email us with:
