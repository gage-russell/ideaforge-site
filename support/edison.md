# Edison (AI)

Use Edison to accelerate your work with agentic AI: generate ideas, research competitors, and explore customer segments.

<div class="card" role="note" aria-label="Edison private preview notice">
  <strong>Private preview:</strong> Edison is currently available as a free private preview for select users. If you’d like to be considered, email <a href="mailto:{{ site.email }}">{{ site.email }}</a>.
</div>

## Open Edison

Tap the robot icon in the top‑right to open Edison.

<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/support/edison/edison-navigation-icon.png" alt="Open Edison from the robot icon" width="360" />

## Overview: Edison workspace

The Edison workspace shows your current conversation, quick prompts, and tools to guide the agent.

<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/support/edison/edison.png" alt="Edison workspace overview" width="360" />

## Open Context & Tools

Click the gearhead icon in the top‑right to expand the Context & Tools dropdown. Use it to attach context (like the current idea or group) and enable agent tools. Edison uses this to reason better and take actions with the right references.

<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/support/edison/edison-context-and-tools.png" alt="Context & Tools dropdown opened" width="360" />

## Quick prompts

Open Quick Prompts by clicking the magic icon to the left of the text input at the bottom. Pick a prompt to jump‑start tasks like competitor research or customer segments.

<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/support/edison/quick-prompts.png" alt="Quick Prompts menu (magic icon)" width="360" />

## Generate an idea

Use the prompt field to ask Edison to draft a new idea. It will synthesize a clear title and summary, and you can refine from there.

<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/support/edison/edison-create-idea.png" alt="Edison generating a new idea" width="360" />

## Research and create competitors — quick prompt

From Quick Prompts, choose “Research & create competitors.” Edison will draft competitor entries with references.

<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/support/edison/edison-research-and-create-competitors-quick-prompt.png" alt="Quick prompt to research and create competitors" width="360" />

## Research and create competitors — result

Edison creates competitor records and returns them with links. Anything produced by an agent tool call is linkable—open items to explore details and sources.

<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/support/edison/edison-create-competitor.png" alt="Competitors created by the agent with linkable results" width="360" />

## Research and create customer segments — quick prompt

From Quick Prompts, choose “Research & create customer segments” to generate segments tailored to your idea.

<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/support/edison/edison-research-and-create-customer-segments-quick-prompt.png" alt="Quick prompt to research and create customer segments" width="360" />

## Research and create customer segments — message

Review or tweak the filled prompt in the input box, then send the message to run the task.

<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/support/edison/edison-research-and-create-customer-segments-quick-prompt-message.png" alt="Customer segments prompt ready to send" width="360" />

## Research and create customer segments — response

Review Edison’s response for segment definitions and next steps. You can refine or ask follow‑ups.

<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/support/edison/edison-research-and-create-customer-segments-response.png" alt="Agent response for customer segments" width="360" />
